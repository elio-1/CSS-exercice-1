Gratin recipe

Very Tasty

Marinade

Rub the duck with salt pepper thym, laurier then put in the fridge for 24hours

Recipe

  • take the duck out of the fridge 30 min before to let it heat to the temperature around.
  • Melt the duck greese with a little bi tof water.
  • Cook the duck inside the melted greese for 10 min
  • Let it cool down and keep inside a hermetic recipient
  • Heat up and cook up for 10 min then enjoy
