Gratin dauphinois recipe

Gratin Dauphinois is a classic French dish made with thinly sliced potatoes and a creamy sauce, often topped with cheese.

Potato & Cream Choices

The potatos and the cream you choose are the most import thing in a gratin. They are the base of this dish. You need 'melting' potatos and high quality cream to make the best gratin. The best potato is Monalisa

Ingredients

  • potatoes: peeled and sliced into 1/8-inch thick rounds
  • Butter: for greasing the baking dish
  • heavy cream
  • milk
  • Salt & pepper
  • A little bit of nutmeg (optional)
  • Garlic (optional)

Recipe

  • Peal and cut the potatos very thin in a circle
  • Cook the potatos in the milk but not fully cook them
  • butter and ail the bottom of the recipient for the gratin
  • stack with: 1 layer of potatos, one of cream, salt, pepper and nutmeg. Repeat until full
  • cook at 200 degrees celcius for 1hour
